General specification:
-This is a product magagement Android application, basically allow small and medium-sized shops to have thier owen magament tool freely.
-After Loging in, if you click blue plus button, it'll bring you to scan barcode with your cemera, so please run it in real-world device.
-5 categories are provided, make sure choose one when adding a new goods.
-If the expiration date is less than or equal to one month from the current time, the item will be displayed in the area of the home page that indicates expired items.
-when you click any item card, you'll see its detailed description and basical operation, such as deleting, editing and gerating a barcode.
